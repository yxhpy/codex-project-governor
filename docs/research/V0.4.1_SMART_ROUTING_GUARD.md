# V0.4.1 Research Brief: Smart Routing Guard

## Problem

v0.4 improves coding speed with task routing and quality gates, but two routing errors are possible:

1. simple local changes may be over-governed and slowed down
2. complex changes may be under-routed and implemented too narrowly

## Decision

Add `micro_patch` routing and `route-guard`.

Speed comes from smart downgrading. Quality comes from automatic escalation when the actual diff exceeds the selected route.
